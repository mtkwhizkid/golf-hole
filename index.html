How to play

Grab near the ball, drag back, let go. Pull further to hit harder.

Drag anywhere else to look around. Use Bird view to navigate the whole hole from above.

Choose a club for full shots. Wind moves the ball in the air.

Rough and sand reduce power. Water and out of bounds send you back with a penalty.

One new full hole every day. Your first finish counts.
